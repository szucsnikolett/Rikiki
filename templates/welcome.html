Welcome to Rikiki!

  • Aim To win the most points by accurately predicting how many tricks you'll get in each round
  • The dealer deals the cards.
  • The dealer takes a card from remaining deck and place it down. That is the trump card.
  • Players begin to bid how many tricks they can take.
  • Play begins: you must place same color as the first player in turn.
  • If you do not have the same color, you can either put a trump or anything.
  • The hand will be won by the highest card or who places trump down.
  • Once all cards have been played, the dealer tallies up the score.
  • If a player takes the exact number of tricks he bid, he receives 10 points, plus twice number he bid.
  • If a player takes any other number of tricks, he will be deducted points.
